Art Unseen

Curated by Jaquan

Curatorial Text

Art Unseen is an online exhibition that seeks to celebrate digital art found across social media platforms. The artworks featured in this exhibition are often unseen gems, created by talented artists who may not receive the recognition they deserve. Through this exhibition, we aim to shed light on the significance of digital art in today's online culture and honor the creativity and skill of its creators.
