Art Unseen

Curated by Jaquan

Curatorial Text

Art Unseen is an online exhibition that celebrates the essence of net art, offering viewers an immersive experience through a collection of visually captivating digital artworks. Stylistically interconnected, the featured artworks provide a cohesive exploration of the vibrant world of net art, each offering a unique perspective on contemporary digital creativity. From dynamic animations to mesmerizing illustrations, these artworks collectively redefine the boundaries of digital expression, inviting audiences to delve into the evocative realms of online culture. Join us on a journey through the interconnected landscapes of net art and discover the unseen beauty that awaits within.
